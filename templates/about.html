About Us

Our History

The Beginning

Anchor University Laundry Venture was founded in 2017 with a vision to revolutionize campus laundry services. What started as a small operation with just two washing machines has grown into a comprehensive laundry service center serving the entire university community.

Our founder, recognizing the need for quality laundry services on campus, established the venture with a commitment to excellence and student convenience. The location was set up near the guest house, strategically positioned to serve both residential and non-residential members of the university community.

Our Journey

  • May 2017: Launched with basic laundry services
  • August 2018: Expanded to include dry cleaning services
  • November 2019: Introduced online booking system
  • January 2020: Upgraded to eco-friendly equipment
  • March 2020: Implemented express delivery service

Why Choose Us?

We pride ourselves on offering the best laundry services on campus with a focus on quality, speed, and customer satisfaction. Our state-of-the-art facilities and eco-friendly practices set us apart.
